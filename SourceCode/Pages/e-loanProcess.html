NMEMPC e-Loan Processing

The e-Loan Processing of San Jose del Monte Savings and Credit Cooperative is ONLY for the members of this Cooperative. To apply for membership, click here. All personal information collected by San Jose del Monte Savings and Credit Cooperative is done so exclusively with your consent, by means of submission on our application form, an email received from you via JotForm. No information is collected automatically.

The personal information collected is only used by the Loan Processor for the purposes defined at the time of the collection or a use that complies with these purposes. We do not share your information with any third parties.

As mentioned above, we use your personal information to appropriately process your applications and present you with the information you need to access. We also use all of the information you provide voluntarily in order to make your loan transactions on San Jose del monte Savings Credit Cooperative possible.

If you agree in this term, we can use your personal information in order to process your loan applications whether to be approved or not.
