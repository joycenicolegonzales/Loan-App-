ABOUT US

MISSION

To improve the quality of life of members by implementing credit programs that will continously sustain their basic and other human needs.
To work together as one for and in behalf of the greater majority of each members with the genuine support and active participation of all stakeholders.
To initiate viable and sustainable socio economic programs for people from all levels of society throught cooperativism and self-reliance.

VISION

Nasugbu Municipal Employees Multi-Purpose Cooperative, as a prosperous cooperative in the First District of Batangas Province, wherein all members and community as well would feel its presence as the number one provider for credit, consumer and marketing services.
